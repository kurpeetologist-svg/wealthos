# WealthOS — Protected Ideas

These ideas are intentionally preserved for later phases. They should not be rushed before the product has the required data and infrastructure.

## Purchase Context

Help users understand what an individual purchase means across time horizons.

Example:

- Coffee spending totals $24 this week.
- That is about 1% of this week's income.
- If a similar purchase happened every weekday, it would total about $150 each month.

Principle:

**Every purchase deserves perspective, not judgment.**

## What If? Purchase Preview

Let a user test a potential purchase before completing it.

## Quick Log

Mobile-first expense entry for purchases made in the moment.

## Daily Spending

Today's spending, today's receipts, and how today rolls into the week and month.

## Financial Confidence

Behavior-based progress without a judgmental numeric score.

## WealthOS Memory

Long-term references to goals, habits, lessons, milestones, and changes.

## Financial Journeys

Debt payoff, emergency funds, home savings, travel, investing, and retirement.

## Contextual Explainers

Country-specific and concept-specific education such as taxes, SSS, Pag-IBIG, PhilHealth, credit utilization, retirement accounts, and insurance.

## Account Connections

Secure automatic updates as a convenience, never a requirement.

## AI Financial Companion

Only after the product has earned enough context, history, and trust.
