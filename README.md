# WealthOS v0.8.4 — Confidence-First Experience Release

WealthOS is a financial literacy product.

## North Star

**WealthOS exists to help people build confidence with money, one decision at a time.**

## Mission

**Make financial wisdom accessible to everyone.**

## Belief

**No one should have to learn money the hard way.**

## Product Promise

**Every time someone opens WealthOS, they should leave with more clarity than they arrived with.**

## Product Philosophy

**Decisions first. Data second.**

## Experience Principle

If a screen does not make the user feel calmer after five seconds than they did before opening it, we redesign it.

If someone closes WealthOS feeling more confused than when they opened it, we have failed our promise.

## Interaction Model

Every important experience follows the same sequence:

1. **Orient me** — Where am I?
2. **Reassure me** — Am I okay?
3. **Focus me** — What matters today?
4. **Teach me** — What does this mean?
5. **Remember me** — How did I get here?

## Product Architecture

- **Lobby** — arrival and reassurance
- **Today’s Focus** — the four most important Signals
- **Timeline** — the financial moments shaping the user’s journey
- **Stories** — why each moment matters

## Language Update

“Today’s Briefing” has been renamed to **Today’s Focus**.

Focus is positive, calm, non-authoritative, and aligned with WealthOS’s purpose: helping users stay on top of their finances without pressure or judgment.

## WealthOS Never Judges

WealthOS observes, explains, and encourages.

Avoid:
- Warning
- Failure
- Bad
- Risk
- Overspent
- Behind

Prefer:
- Attention
- Worth reviewing
- Changed
- Still building
- Room to grow
- Next Step

## Scope of v0.8.4

This release does not add new calculations or financial features. It aligns the stable product experience with the official WealthOS philosophy and language.
